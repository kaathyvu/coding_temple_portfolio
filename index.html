From Veterinary Pharmacy to Programming

Hello, world! To introduce myself, my name is Kathy Vu and I am an aspiring software developer. Before I got to this point, I was actually a Veterinary Pharmacist. I went to MCPHS University for six years to get my PharmD., and then worked full-time as a Veterinary Pharmacist for another five years. Although my work was very rewarding, I discovered I was never truly happy with my career. I often felt like I was just going through the motions and I mainly stayed with it because I was comfortable. However, in order to grow, I knew that I'd have to face change and discomfort. When the pandemic initially hit, I took the time to explore programming, and I taught myself some basic coding. To my surprise, I found myself actually enjoying it, which was a weird feeling because I had never felt that with pharmacy. After a lot of support and encouragement from my family and friends, I decided to leave the pharmacy world and make a career change to programming. To say I'm anxious is an understatement, however, I'm more excited to see this next chapter in my life unfold.

Goals for 2022

My biggest goals for 2022 are to say yes to more things in life and to get out of my comfort zone more often. I am someone who thrives on daily routines and set schedules, but I am realizing that some of the best adventures and memories of mine are the ones that are least expected. I don't want to live a life filled with "could haves" or "should haves" and the only way to do that is to try new things and to not be afraid to face a little discomfort sometimes.
